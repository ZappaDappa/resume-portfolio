About Me

Caleb McMillan is an on-air personality with 89.5 the Lake in Kenora, ON. Previously, he could be heard on 88.7 The River in Mount Forest. Caleb worked for over a decade as a freelance writer, ghostwriting novels, and teaming up with breweries, cannabis growers, overhead door operators, and YouTubers. Now, he's back to his first love - radio.
